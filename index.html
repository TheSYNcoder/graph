Graph Generator

General Instructions

This is a graph generating website useful for visualizing graphs during competitive programming. You are required to enter Number of nodes and Number of edges in a single line. Say those are n and m . Now following you should enter m lines each containing two integers u and v where u and v detects that there is an undirected edge, and each of u and v should be less than n. Please note all data should be numeric and must be integers !
